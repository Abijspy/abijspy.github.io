Project Overview

The system provides the administrator with complete access to student academic records, attendance, grades, and payments. Data is stored in a secure cloud-managed SQLite database, ensuring both privacy and accessibility.

Key modules include:

  • Academics: Track academic terms, courses, and performance.
  • Attendance: Record and monitor attendance to determine exam eligibility.
  • Marks & Grades: Automatically calculate and display results.
  • Payments: Enable parents to pay academic fees online with generated bills.

By integrating frontend and backend technologies, the system offers a streamlined, full-stack solution for student and faculty data management.
