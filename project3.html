Project Overview

The main source of water is dam or river water, which is purified and tested for diseases before being sent to storage tanks for distribution. A dedicated monitoring room tracks leakage, water delivery, and unauthorized extraction.

Wastewater from households is purified and stored in a recycled water tank for reuse in gardening and other non-drinking purposes. Rainwater harvesting systems are implemented in every household and main tank to store water for scarcity periods.

This system regulates water pressure, quality, and pollution levels — making it suitable for smart cities, towns, and villages.
